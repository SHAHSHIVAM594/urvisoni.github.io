Tech-Top | National Innovation & Entrepreneurship Challenge

  • taken by:rajesh nair
  • date:8 July to 15 July, 2017
 

Tech-Top is a not-for-profit non-governmental initiative with the goal of identifying, promoting and celebrating young innovators from rural and urban India, TechTop Charitable Trust has been organising national annual TechTop Innovation Challenge since 2006. Prof. Rajesh Nair (MIT Scholar) will held and take one week workshop on Designing, Innovation and Enterpreneurship. I am selected in this workshop in July 2017. In 2017, it was held at IIT-Delhi.

In this Workshop I learned So many things like Arduino, 3-D Printing, Pitching, Design-Thinking, Ideation, Prototyping, Presentation also I learned that How to work with Team, How to Solve Day-to-Day Life and People's Problem. Mentors Give Us a Particular Topic for Solving Problem So that We are gone to "Tamnna High School" Which is in Delhi For Physical and Mental Ability of Students.

I meet Different Countries, Different States People, MITians, IITians, Enterprinour and Learn from them some things like How to manage, How to Pitch in Public, How to Create Own Start-Ups etc. People Who are Working and Manager, Mentor Of Tech-Top they are So Down-to-Earth People and Give their Knowlege, Share Their Experince With Us, Motivate Us And Give their Suggesion, Advice For future Goal of Students.
